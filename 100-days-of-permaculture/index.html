Day one 21/05/22

Today was day one of 100 days of Permaculture!!!
I’ve been working on my Diploma in Applied Permaculture since 2019, and I’m halfway through. I’ve got to raise my game as I’ve just been given the go ahead to do my accreditation event at this year’s National Permaculture Gathering. Yoiks! So, to keep up the momentum, I’m going to do a 100 day challenge. I’ll be posting every day, until the event. There’s more than 100 days but I’m off on retreat, but I can still fit in 100).
Luckily, I’m at a lovely farm @Brookside.eco.farm where Permaculture is very much embraced, and it looks as though we might be staying! I’ll be doing at least one design for them, there’s lots of exciting stuff happening!
These pictures are of the incredible WET system they had installed. It cleans all the grey and brown water from the main buildings, as it passes through five ponds. The water is cleaned by diverse range of trees and perennials, many of which provide food or other uses. By the fifth pond, it’s clean enough to swim in!
Not only that, walking through it is heavenly.
Also, Benji has become a reluctant member of a pack of four dogs, this is Trewa. It was quite a challenge to get a shot of them together.
Today I finished the amendments for my first five designs. I’m taking on a big challenge, but I’m going to give it everything I’ve got. I hope you’ll enjoy following my progress-wish me luck!!
