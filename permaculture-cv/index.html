Permaculture CV

Profile

Permaculture has been on my radar for twenty-five years, as someone in the community I lived in back then was growing turnips in a geodesic dome, and I remember the turnip grower saying he was using permaculture methods. I've always loved turnips too...Then I heard a bit about it when I was in Australia a couple of years later, but really only started to get interested in it when I did a bit of Wwoofing in 2015. Then I did the introduction workshop and that set the ball rolling.

Now I want to integrate it into my daily life, work and everything. Including turnip growing.
