Context

A design to help myself and Killian find a way to the life that we aspire to

I started this design during the first Lockdown. It has been on the backburner since, but there has been some progress and quite a few tweaks. 

And, when you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it.


― Paulo Coelho, The Alchemist

Introduction

I remember being asked by the guy in the job centre when I was 17, where I saw myself in five years’ time!! Hilarious, given that within a year I’d be in a cult, and wouldn’t leave for another six years!

Well, I am asking myself where I’d like to be in five years’ time now. In 10 years’ time, I’d like to be living on the land, with some good folk, fellow guardians of the land. I’d like to be shit-hot at growing food and be skilled in other practical stuff – preserving, cooking, foraging etc. I’d like to have zero carbon footprint. In five years’ time, I’d like to be well on my way to being skilled up. And I’d like to be ridiculously happy. Actually, the latter is the most important…

I would like to do a design that is about how to earn money/get by, perhaps on a system that is not about money…How about being community sufficient? I would like to live in close harmony with the land. At the moment, I live in a second floor flat in Baildon, on the edge of Shipley. It is very much an urban situation. It's quite a far call from where I'd like to be, but **INSERT PRINCIPLE HERE!**

I feel anxious about this endeavour, as though I need to be out there already, learning. I am worried that I should be getting on with looking for a placement…but I am here, now. Perhaps it would be good to enjoy a year working on my diploma, get a part time job, enjoy the allotment, wait for Covid to pass – if it will…sit out the storm. As Burgs said, now is not the time to be venturing forth into new things…chill, make a full recovery from the frozen shoulder and use the time wisely. Get fit again. Look at the list of skills I’d like to acquire and see if I can do any from where I am now.

 

In re-writing it, I felt that I wanted to change the framework, as it's an ongoing design.
