4. Being Well

Overview

Aim

To develop a process that helps me come to terms with my health issues and to heal where I can.

Me

Me

Context

As a means to dealing with various health issues, I decided to create a design that would help me navigate my way through chronic pain, and all consequences of this. I started it in January 2020, when I was suffering from a frozen shoulder. Healing is a slow process. The design has evolved and I have re-worked it where I felt it would help me in my current state of health.

 

This design process was a very personal one. I wouldn't want to put anyone through having to read it, other than the assessors - they're getting paid.

So if you're interested, I've whittled it down into a little presentation that you can watch.
